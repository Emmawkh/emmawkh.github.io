What was expected and my vision of the project

This project was realized as part of an end of year rendering for my Bachelor degree in Digital Project Management.
By taking the instructions of renderings, I decided to orient this project as a portfolio site that can serve me later, but also to share my future creations.

The difficulties of this project

The first difficulty that I could meet during this project, it was mainly the knowledge of putting on line a site.
Being a web development major, I wanted to code the project from A to Z, but I didn't know how to put it online

Then, I met some difficulties as for example trying to make a site as light as possible to the digital, to have a fast and easy to load site.

And finally, I added an additional difficulty, to make the site entirely in English !
