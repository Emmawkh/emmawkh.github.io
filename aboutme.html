About
me

After my studies in retailing, I wanted to do something completely different, but I knew I wanted to do something with my creativity.
That’s when I discovered web design and web integration. Since then and along with my studies (and because I don’t like to make choices when it come to preferences), I want to merge this two skills that I love so much. To me, one doesn’t exist without the other when we want to do a job that matters.

I am adaptable to all kinds of situations and am a jack-of-all-trades, passionate about many things and especially curious about many subjects.
